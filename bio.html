Biography

My name is Peter Quinn. I am studying Honours Electrical Engineering with a minor in Physics at McGill University. This is my website, where I keep a portfolio of projecs that I've worked on and things that I've done.

I've been a student at McGill university since Fall 2015, and I am due to graduate in Winter of 2019. I chose to study electrical engineering because I have always enjoyed working with and learning about math, physics, and computers. The electrical engineering program at McGill combines all of those subjects, and I was further able to specialize my degree by taking the minor in physics. I opted for the honours program since it will allow me to do a thesis research project rather than design project as my capstone project. I plan to pursue graduate studies after my undergrad so this seemed like a good opportunity to learn and gain experience in a research setting early.
