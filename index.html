About me

I currently work full time for Torc Robotics, an autonomous trucking company. I work on the calibration team for the fleet of test vehicles, particapting in the intrinsic and extrinsic calibration of various sensors (cameras, LiDARs, Radars, GPS/IMU).

My day to day typically involves writing code (Python, C++) to calibrate and integrate different sensors, monitor their performance, and analyze any issues.

During my free time, I like working on side projects involving Arduinos/Raspberri Pis, AI, and 3D printing. When I am not doing that, I am usually playing D&D or guitar.
